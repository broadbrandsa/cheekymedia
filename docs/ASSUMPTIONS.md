# Assumptions

This proposal is directional and built from publicly available information about
Cheeky Media. The following assumptions apply and require validation after approval.

## Data & access

- **No internal data access.** We have not been given analytics, CMS access,
  hosting credentials, or existing brand asset files.
- Content shown in the proposal is reconstructed from the public site
  (https://cheekymediaworld.com) and is **directional**, not final.
- Portfolio items, team names and project references are taken from the live site
  and must be confirmed for accuracy and rights to reuse.

## Strategy

- The rebuild strategy presented here is **directional only**. Final IA, copy and
  visual direction will be confirmed in a discovery session post-approval.
- Pricing (R30,000) assumes the scope defined in `SCOPE.md`. Material changes to
  scope (e-commerce, bespoke integrations, ongoing retainers) are out of scope and
  quoted separately.

## Technical

- Client can provide (or delegate) a domain and DNS access for go-live.
- Cheeky Media supplies the **CI / brand guidelines, logo files and work-done
  (portfolio) assets**. Copy may be supplied by the client or written by Broadbrand
  on request; stock photography can be sourced by Broadbrand where needed.
- Third-party services (forms, analytics, email) use standard free/low-cost tiers
  unless the client requests otherwise.

## Requires validation post-approval

1. Confirm full service list and any new offerings to feature.
2. Confirm brand colours, typography and logo files.
3. Confirm portfolio pieces, permissions, and any NDAs.
4. Confirm contact details, social links, and lead-routing email.
5. Confirm hosting, domain and analytics ownership.
